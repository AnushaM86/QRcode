Hackathon Problem Statements

Total Problem Statements: 4

1. Environmental Hazard Detection App

Build an app that detects and reports environmental hazards in urban areas. The app should provide real-time alerts and allow users to report hazardous conditions.

2. Traffic Flow Optimization Solution

Design a solution that optimizes traffic flow in crowded cities using machine learning algorithms. The solution should dynamically adjust traffic signals and provide real-time suggestions to drivers.

3. Volunteering Opportunity Finder

Create a web application that helps people find and register for nearby volunteering opportunities based on their skills, location, and availability.

4. Mental Health AI Chatbot

Develop an AI-based chatbot that assists users with mental health challenges. The chatbot should provide resources, coping strategies, and support to users in need.
